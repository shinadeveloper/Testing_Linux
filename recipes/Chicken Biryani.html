Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice. For my Biryani, I simplify the order of operations, while retaining the traditional layered approach to assembling it. This recipe captures the essence of how I like to make my Biryani, and I hope you find it to be a delicious and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint
  • 1/2 cup fried onions
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground fennel
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground fenugreek
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground onion
