Noodles

Noodles are a versatile and delicious dish that can be made in a variety of ways. They can be stir-fried, boiled, or served in a soup, and can be made with a variety of ingredients. Noodles are a great way to use up any leftover vegetables or protein you have on hand, and they come together quickly and easily. This recipe is simple and customizable, so feel free to add or omit any ingredients you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
